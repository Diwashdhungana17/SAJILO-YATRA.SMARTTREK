Popular Tourist Destinations in Nepal

Mount Everest

The world's highest peak at 8,848 meters, Everest is the ultimate destination for trekkers and mountaineers. The Everest Base Camp trek offers stunning views and a chance to experience Sherpa culture.

Explore

Pokhara

Nepal's second largest city, known for its stunning lakes and panoramic views of the Annapurna mountain range. Popular for paragliding, boating, and as a gateway to the Annapurna Circuit trek.

Explore

Kathmandu Valley

The cultural heart of Nepal, home to seven UNESCO World Heritage Sites including Swayambhunath (Monkey Temple), Pashupatinath, and Durbar Squares. A vibrant mix of ancient traditions and modern life.

Explore

Chitwan National Park

Nepal's first national park and a UNESCO World Heritage Site. Famous for jungle safaris where you can spot rhinos, elephants, and if lucky, the Bengal tiger.

Explore

Lumbini

The birthplace of Lord Buddha and a UNESCO World Heritage Site. A sacred pilgrimage site with monasteries from various Buddhist countries and the ancient Mayadevi Temple.

Explore

Annapurna Circuit

One of the world's classic treks, circling the Annapurna Massif. The route passes through diverse landscapes from subtropical valleys to high mountain deserts.
